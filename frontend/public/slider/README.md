# Slider Images Guide

## Required Images

Please add the following 4 images to this folder:

### 1. slide1.jpg
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Subject**: Litein Municipality building or town center
- **Usage**: Welcome slide
- **Alt suggestions**: Town hall, municipal building, aerial view of Litein

### 2. slide2.jpg
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Subject**: Citizens using services or technology
- **Usage**: Citizen Services Online slide
- **Alt suggestions**: Person using computer/mobile, service desk, digital services

### 3. slide3.jpg
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Subject**: Business/investment scene
- **Usage**: Investment Opportunities slide
- **Alt suggestions**: Business meeting, modern buildings, development projects

### 4. slide4.jpg
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Subject**: Community engagement or reporting
- **Usage**: Report Issues slide
- **Alt suggestions**: Community meeting, citizens talking, service center

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Keep file size under 500KB for faster loading
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (landscape)
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080px for HD displays
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit, clear subjects
- **Content**: Professional photos that represent Litein Municipality positively

## Where to Get Images

1. **Local Photos**: Take photos of Litein town, municipal buildings, and services
2. **Stock Photos**: Use royalty-free images from:
   - Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
   - Pexels (https://pexels.com)
   - Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)
3. **County Archives**: Check Kericho County photo archives
